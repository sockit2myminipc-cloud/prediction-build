This is a standalone prediction market research and automated betting engine. It is a different project from the trading bot — where the trading bot uses prediction markets as one signal among many, this system treats prediction markets as the primary market. News, statistics, and cross-platform arbitrage are the signal layer on top.

Vegas / exchange odds (sports arbitrage)

PHASE 2
News Ingestion & NLP
Detect the gap between news breaking and markets repricing
The core edge in prediction market betting is information arbitrage — news breaks, the market hasn't repriced yet, and you have a 5–
30 minute
 window to act. This phase builds the engine that detects those windows automatically.
ℹ️  How
 the news lag works
1. Breaking news event is published (e.g. Fed chair makes surprise comment)
2. Your system ingests the article within 60-120 seconds of publication
3. NLP extracts entities: 'Fed', 'interest rates', 'Powell', 'March'
4. Entity matcher scans all open markets for keyword overlap
5. For each matched market: 
checks
 if probability has moved in last 30 min
6. If price is FLAT but news is clearly relevant → ALERT FIRED
   The window: news is out, crowd hasn't priced it yet. This is your edge.

PHASE 3
Statistical Baselines & Outlier Detection
Find markets where the crowd is provably mispriced
The second edge source is statistical arbitrage — comparing what the prediction market says against what independent models say. When they diverge significantly, one of them is wrong. Your job is to identify which one and bet accordingly.
Market Type
Baseline Source
Outlier Threshold
Sports
The Odds API (free tier), ESPN stats
±8% vs Vegas line
Elections
Wikipedia poll aggregates, FiveThirtyEight archive
